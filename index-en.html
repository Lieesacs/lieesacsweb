About Liée Sacs

Liée Sacs was born from the desire to create unique handbags combining elegance, delicacy, and comfort. Each piece is carefully designed and handcrafted in Barcelona, using carefully selected materials to ensure quality, durability, and style. Our crochet bags are 100% handmade and made to order, allowing us to focus on every detail and offer a truly exclusive product. The creation process takes between 1 and 2 weeks, reflecting the time and dedication we put into each design.
