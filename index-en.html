About Liée Sacs

Liée Sacs was born from the desire to create unique bags combining elegance, delicacy, and comfort. Each piece is carefully designed and handmade in Barcelona, using materials carefully selected to guarantee quality, durability, and style. Our crochet bags are 100% handmade and made to order, allowing attention to every detail and offering a truly exclusive product. The creation process takes between 1 and 2 weeks, reflecting the time and dedication we put into each design.
